About the Lab

Who We Are

The NextGen Biomaterials Lab, under the leadership of Dr. Alok Kumar, is focused on advancing the field of biodegradable bone implants. We integrate mechanical engineering with biomedical innovation to create sustainable and high-impact solutions for the healthcare industry.

About Dr. Alok Kumar

Dr. Alok Kumar is a dynamic Mechanical Engineer with a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering, specializing in Biodegradable Bone Implants. A graduate of MNNIT Allahabad, he brings 4-5 years of industry and academic experience. His work stands at the intersection of engineering, sustainability, and innovation.

Mission

To lead pioneering research in biodegradable materials and transform orthopedic care with innovative, eco-friendly engineering solutions.
