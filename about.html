About the Lab

The NextGen Biomaterials Lab (NGBL) is a student-driven, faculty-mentored research unit focused on pushing the boundaries of biomaterials science.

Our mission is to bridge biology, materials science, and engineering to create next-generation solutions in tissue engineering, biodegradable polymers, and nanoscale delivery systems. We promote open innovation, sustainability, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Based at Uttaranchal University, the lab acts as a launchpad for undergraduate innovation and research-driven learning experiences.
