About Dr. Alok Kumar

Pronouns: He/Him

Specializations: Biomaterials, In-Vivo & In-Vitro Testing, Additive Manufacturing, Medical Devices & Implants

Dr. Alok Kumar is a dynamic Mechanical Engineer with a Ph.D. focused on biodegradable bone implants. With advanced degrees from institutions like MNNIT Allahabad and over 4 years of research experience, his work bridges sustainable innovation and biomedical engineering. His research emphasizes eco-friendly, cutting-edge solutions in medical device development.
