Our Research

At the NextGen Biomaterials Lab, our research is focused on the development of innovative materials that have the potential to revolutionize the medical and engineering fields. We are currently exploring:

  • Smart Biomaterials - materials that can adapt and respond to their environment.
  • Regenerative Medicine - scaffolds and hydrogels to aid tissue regeneration.
  • Biocompatible Composites - combinig sysnthetic and natural polymers for safe and effective use.
  • 3D Printing for Biomedical Applications - creating complex structures for implants and tissue engineering.

We combine experimental work with computational mofeling to gain deeper insight and drive innovation in material science.
