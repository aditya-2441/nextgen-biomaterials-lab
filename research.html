Our Research

Biodegradable Magnesium Alloys

Investigating the mechanical and corrosion properties of magnesium-based alloys to develop porous structures for biodegradable orthopedic implants.

Bone Implant Scaffolding

Designing and optimizing scaffold geometries to mimic bone porosity and enhance cellular growth using 3D printing and simulation tools.

Corrosion Behavior & Surface Treatment

Studying the degradation profile of bio-implants and applying surface modifications to control corrosion rate for clinical safety.

Interdisciplinary Collaborations

Collaborating with bioengineers, chemists, and medical experts to integrate materials science with real-world healthcare challenges.
